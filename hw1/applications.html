AI in Daily Life

Artificial Intelligence is used in many areas of modern life. It helps computers make predictions, recognize patterns, and support decisions based on data.


Common Uses of AI

Healthcare

AI can help analyze medical images, organize patient data, and support doctors when making decisions.

Education

AI can support tutoring systems, language learning tools, and personalized practice for students.

Cybersecurity

AI can help detect suspicious activity, identify possible threats, and protect computer systems.
